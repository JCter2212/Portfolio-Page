Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio page! Hopefully it will provide valuable insight into what skills and capabilities I can bring to the table.

Consider me a multi-tool in the business world. The majority of my experience is related to business data analysis, compliance, quality auditing, project management and process development. Fundamentally these fields topics required dual functionality as first an analyst and then an analytics manager. Whether developing Power BI reports, managing projects, or presenting audit results to management or clients, the work is data driven.

Visualizations

Power BI: Office Supply Store

Retail Operations--a data rich topic we all come across. How well does a product sell? What region generates the most revenue? How many customers are buying a particular product? Answer these questions and more with the Office Store Report. Project details available on GitHub.

Power BI: Global Information Report

How do nations stack up in terms of economics, development, and environmental impact? This report provides insight into major areas of concern for six nations on the world stage. Drill down deeper into factors contributing to topics such as GDP, HDI, and Environmental impact. View the full project in GitHub.

Data Cleaning and Analysis

Excel: Bike Sales

Perhaps the most versatile of all of the tools in the analyst tool belt, excel does it all. Ad hoc analysis, data cleaning, visualization--Excel can do it all. Besides, no matter how well developed the Power BI report, someone will always ask for the data in a spreadsheet.

This report includes examples of manual data cleaning, VBA data cleaning, piovt tables, and basic visualization. Full project on GitHub.

SQL: Layoffs

Something akin to "old faithful" in the data world, SQL remains a staple in many data projects. After all, what good is a Power BI report without data from the source? Whether data cleaning at the query level or ad hoc analysis is needed ASAP, SQL is often the way to go. Full project on GitHub.

SQL: Gym Memberships

Another SQL project, this time focused on data cleaning and re-loading the table. Find the analysis in the Tableau Dashboard Gym Memberships. Full project on GitHub.
